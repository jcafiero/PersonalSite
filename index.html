About Me

My name is Jennifer Cafiero and I am an aspiring web developer. Currently, I am working toward my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken, NJ. Over these last three years, I have spent time learning a variety of topics from Discrete Math to Data Structures to complex Algorithms to Web Programming.

Personally, I feel like I always knew I wanted to go into computing on some level. Thirteen-year-old me even decided to apply to an Information Technology career academy, where I spent my high school years learning Project Management, Databasement Management, and Java Programming skills, while also taking the core requirement classes necessary for graduation. What also piqued my interest, was how broad the field of Computer Science is. This made me want to look for a school with a well-established Cooperative Education program, which would let me explore internships during my college career. This is what led me to Stevens.

During my time at Stevens, I have had the opportunity to have three internship cycles with two enriching employers. My first internship was at Movado Group, Inc. where I was on the IT team and supported the warehouse carousels and other hardware. My last two internships have been at AB Global where I have been a Front-End Developer for the Marketing Team.

I hope you enjoy exploring my resume through this site!
